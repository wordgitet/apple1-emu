Codetank_GAME4.rom — TMS9918 P-LAB CodeTank cartridge
  Lower jumper: 4000R → TMS_LightCorridor (wireframe perspective
                tunnel + paddle + ball, 3 difficulty levels).
                Controls: A/Q D = left/right, W/Z S = up/down,
                SPACE = launch, ESC = exit to Wozmon.
                Original implementation inspired by Infogrames'
                Light Corridor (1990) concept — original code,
                original assets.
  Upper jumper: reserved ($FF fill — future expansion).
